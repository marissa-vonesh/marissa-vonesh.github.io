about

As a recent graduate of the University of Miami, I have years of experience working for the university’s student lifestyle magazine, Distraction, having served as both the editor-in-chief and art director. I’ve had stints fact-checking, researching, writing and curating newsletters for Smithsonian Magazine and 5280 Magazine, developing the brand identity and marketing materials for Amy Foundation, a non-profit in Cape Town, and concepting advertising campaigns and producing creative content for various clients (such as University of Miami’s Herbert Business School and the NCAA) while working for a student-run creative consultancy, Orange Umbrella. I like having my hands in all different pots, working in collaborative, team-oriented environments and using dance breaks as a method of stress relief. I am a creative, a storyteller, a designer and a writer.

Oh, and my name is Marissa.
