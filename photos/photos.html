photography

I have had a camera in my hand since I can remember. Family vacations, outings with friends, hikes -- no matter the circumstance you can find me with my DSLR or pocket digital camera trying to capture precious moments. Just like with my design and writing, I attempt to tell a story through my photography and help others see the beauty in people, places and things.
